Chicken And Rice

How To Make Chicken and Rice

You will learn how to make a simple and tasty recipe packed with protien for the gains. By the end of the recipe will you have a amazing dish of Chick and Rice for your enjoyment!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 Tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
